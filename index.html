Ist International Conference on "Applied Nonlinear Science Computation and Applications"

(Hybrid Mode)

May 13-14, 2024 at ANAND-ICE, Jaipur, Rajasthan, INDIA

About the Conference

We are pleased to announce that the 1st International Conference on "Applied Nonlinear Science Computation and Applications"(ICANSCA2024) will be held at Anand International College of Engineering, Jaipur, India, from May 13 to 14, 2024.

It is the 1st International Conference on "Applied Nonlinear Science Computation and Applications" under the series of conferences between Urgench State University, Uzbekistan, and Anand International College of Engineering, Jaipur, India.

It is an exciting event bridging the gap between theoretical concepts and practical applications in nonlinear science. This conference will serve as a platform for researchers, scholars, and practitioners to exchange ideas, present their latest findings, and explore potential collaborations.

Papers are accepted on the following topics:

  • Potential theory in nonlinear sciences and probability theory:
    • Potential theory
    • Special functions
    • Fractional calculus
    • Probability theory

    • Generating functions
  • Modern problems of mathematical physics:
    • Nonlinear evolution equations
    • Spectral theory for differential and difference operators
    • Direct and inverse problems
    • Integrable Systems
    • Solitons theory
    • Nonlinear differential equations and their applications
  • Functional materials and applications:
    • Computer simulation, sputtering, cluster, ion bombardment, ion-atom collision
    • Functional materials, degradation effects, semiconductor devices, nanoelectronics, 2D materials
    • Solis solution
    • Cold plasma
    • Solar energy, renewable energy sources, DFT simulation
    • Laser plasma physics, laser matter interaction, laser nanotechnology, optics & photonics
  • Artificial intelligence and cybersecurity:
    • Math tools in artificial intelligence
    • Number theory in cybersecurity
    • Natural language processing
    • Computational linguistics and artificial intelligence applications

About Applied Nonlinear Science Lab (ANSL), ANAND-ICE

Applied Nonlinear Science Lab (ANSL), established by the Anand International College of Engineering, India, and International Academy of Visual Arts and Engineering, UK. This is an interdisciplinary research group Lab. ANSL will be initially a group of Undergraduate students, Ph.D. students, and Postdoctoral students working under the Lab. Later will be expanded into a large group consisting of students and researchers from many other universities around the globe.

Traveling

By Road: Jaipur is located on National Highway No.8 connecting Delhi and Mumbai. National Highway 12 links Jaipur with Kota and National Highway 11 links Bikaner with Agra passing through Jaipur. RSRTC operates bus service to major cities in Rajasthan, New Delhi, Uttar Pradesh, Haryana, Madhya Pradesh, Maharashtra, Punjab and Gujarat. City buses are operated by Jaipur City Transport Services Limited (JCTSL) of RSRTC. The service operates more than 400 regular and low-floor buses. Major bus depots are located at Vaishali Nagar, Vidyadhar Nagar and Sanganer.

By Train: Jaipur is the headquarters of North Western Zone of India n Railways. Jaipur Junction railway station is well connected to all major cities of INDIA like Delhi, Mumbai, Hyderabad, Kolkata, Chennai, Bengaluru, Indore, Lucknow and Ahmedabad. Other stations include Gandhinagar, Durgapura, Jagatpura, Ninad Benad and Sanganer. Few important train from New Delhi 19032 Yoga Express, 14659 Delhi-Jaisalmer Express,12958 Rajdhani.

By Metro: Phase-1A is operational between Mansarovar and Chandpole consisting of 9 stations namely Mansarovar, New Aatish Market, Vivek Vihar, Shyam Nagar, Ram Nagar, Civil Lines, Railway Station, Sindhi Camp and Chandpole. Phase-1B is under construction.

By Air: Jaipur International Airport is in Sanganer, 10 km (6 miles) from the centre. Jaipur Airport also provides air cargo services. During winter, sometimes flights towards Indira Gandhi International Airport are diverted to Jaipur Airport due to heavy fog in Delhi. The airport operates regular domestic services to major INDIA n cities including Ahmedabad, Bengaluru, Chandigarh, Raipur, Chennai, Delhi, Guwahati, Hyderabad, Indore, Kolkata, Lucknow, Mumbai, Pune, Surat, Udaipur and Varanasi. International destinations served include Dubai, Muscat, Bangkok, Sharjah and Kuala Lumpur.
